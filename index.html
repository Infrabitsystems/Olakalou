Welcome to Ol Kalou TVC

Shaping the Future Innovators

Ol Kalou Technical and Vocational College is a public TVET institution located in Nyandarua County. We are dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality technical education that equips our graduates with practical skills for the modern job market.

Our commitment is to foster an environment of innovation, discipline, and excellence, transforming youth into competent professionals.
