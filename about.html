Our History & Background

Ol Kalou Technical and Vocational College is a public TVET institution established to provide quality technical, vocational, and entrepreneurship training. Situated in the serene environment of Nyandarua County, the college was built with the vision of becoming a center of excellence in skills development.

We are fully accredited by the Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority (TVETA) and our programs are listed by the Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS).

The college focuses on nurturing innovators and problem solvers who will contribute to the realization of Kenya's Vision 2030 and the global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of excellence in Technical, Vocational and Entrepreneurship Training for sustainable development.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical, vocational and entrepreneurship training, research and innovation to produce competent graduates for the global market.
