How to Apply

Ol Kalou TVC offers multiple intakes throughout the year. We admit students based on merit and the availability of resources. You can apply through KUCCPS or directly to the college.

1. KUCCPS Placement

Students who have recently completed their KCSE can apply to join Ol Kalou TVC through the Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS) portal when the system opens.

2. Direct Application

You can apply directly by filling out the online application form below or downloading the form, filling it, and dropping it at our admissions office.

Intake Periods

  • January Intake: Applications open from November to December.
  • May Intake: Applications open from March to April.
  • September Intake: Applications open from July to August.

General Requirements

  • Copy of KCSE Certificate or Result Slip
  • Copy of National ID or Birth Certificate
  • Copy of Leaving Certificate
  • 2 Passport size photographs
